Ordinary Citizens

Participation Feed

First and foremost, the Better Democracy Portal is a place for ordinary citizens to learn and find opportunities to impact policy to improve their communities. The participation feed recommends the best and latest opportunities in the places you follow. Check out the feed.

Ballot Decider

The ballot decider tool presents important background information and projections for ballot measures, as well as a tool to help you visualize your decision process. Check out an example.

Public Servants

Land Use Planning Outreach

Doing outreach for a land use planning project? Need to get feedback from constituents? Run a survey using our land use planning outreach tool. Contact us to sign up for FREE access for your city.

City Budget Transparency

Want to save time answering constituent questions about your budget's basics? Want your constituents to be informed and able to make smart decisions on local ballot measures? Want beautiful visuals to explore your city's spending without spending six figures? Try out our city budget transparency tool. Check out an example, or contact us to sign up for FREE access for your city.
